About Flavio

Who is Flavio?

Flavio Fernández was born in Murcia on 30th of June 2000. He is the son of the famous Spanish singer, Flavio Augusto Fernández Nicolás. He is known for being part of the Spanish version of the reality show "Operación Triunfo".

Musical career

  • He started playing the piano at the age of 8.
  • At the age of 18 he began self-taught to play the guitar and the trumpet.
  • Two years ago when he decided to make himself known by uploading covers to the network and composing his own songs.
  • In 2019 he entered the Operación Triunfo academy becoming the second finalist of the program.
